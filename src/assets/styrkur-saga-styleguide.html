Brand Identity

Styrkur Saga
Tagline: "Jarn Gera Starkr" (Iron Makes Strong)

Styrkur Saga is a digital embodiment of strength forged through effort — a personal mythology for the modern era. The name combines two Icelandic words: styrkur, meaning "strength," and saga, referring not just to a story, but a journey steeped in meaning, challenge, and transformation. It reflects the truth that strength isn't inherited — it's earned through consistent effort, setbacks, victories, and the quiet work done when no one is watching.

Rooted in Scandinavian minimalism with a distinctly Nordic twist, the visual identity avoids fantasy clichés while embracing the elemental power of the north. The palette is deep and restrained: blackened charcoal, raw metal, evergreen forest, old wood, blood red, and ocean blue — each chosen not only for contrast and tone, but for symbolism. Subtle textures and subdued gradients reflect the world of natural materials: forged iron, carved wood, ancient stone. Together, these build an environment that feels grounded and mythic without being theatrical — a fitting frame for the journey of self-discipline and strength.

Motion Guidelines:
  • Subtle and purposeful animations that enhance rather than distract
  • Norse mythology-inspired effects (forge, runestone, aurora)
  • Performance-conscious with smooth 60fps targets
  • Contextual timing based on user actions and achievements
Implementation Guidelines:
  • Colors: All context families include shades 300→900 for UI states and data visualization
  • Typography: Cinzel for headers/branding, Inter for all UI and body text
  • Gradients: Norse Gold gradients permitted subtly for enhanced visual hierarchy
  • Spacing: Strict 8px grid system for consistent rhythm and alignment
  • Accessibility: High contrast ratios maintained across all color combinations
  • Performance: Optimized for smooth animations and responsive interactions
